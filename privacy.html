每日一卦 · 隐私政策

生效日期：2026年7月8日

「每日一卦」（下称"本应用"）尊重并保护用户隐私。

我们收集的信息

本应用不收集、不上传、不分享任何可识别您身份的个人信息。您的卦象记录、所问之事、心愿内容、签到记录与福币余额均只保存在您的设备本地（及您的 iCloud 设备备份中），开发者无法访问。

邀请好友功能

若您使用"邀请好友"功能，本应用会通过 Apple CloudKit 存储您的匿名 CloudKit 用户标识、邀请码及兑换关系，仅用于发放邀请奖励，不含姓名、联系方式等任何身份信息，也不用于任何形式的追踪或营销。数据存储于 Apple iCloud 服务器。

内购与订阅

会员订阅与福币购买通过 Apple App Store 完成，支付信息由 Apple 处理，本应用不接触任何支付数据。订阅可随时在 App Store 账户设置中管理或取消。

通知

若您授权通知权限，本应用仅用其在设备本地提醒「上香完成」，不涉及任何远程推送服务器。

第三方服务

本应用不集成任何第三方分析、广告或追踪 SDK。

儿童隐私

本应用不面向13岁以下儿童，亦不收集任何用户数据。

Daily Hexagram · Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 8, 2026

Information We Collect

Daily Hexagram does not collect, upload, or share any personally identifiable information. Your readings, questions, wishes, check-in history and coin balance are stored only on your device (and in your own iCloud device backup). The developer has no access to them.

Invite Friends Feature

If you use the Invite Friends feature, the app stores your anonymous CloudKit user identifier, invite code, and redemption relationship via Apple CloudKit, solely to grant invite rewards. This contains no name, contact details, or other identity information, and is never used for tracking or marketing. Data resides on Apple iCloud servers.

Purchases & Subscriptions

Subscriptions and coin purchases are processed by the Apple App Store; the app never touches payment data. Manage or cancel subscriptions anytime in your App Store account settings.

Notifications

If you grant notification permission, it is used solely for local "incense finished" reminders. No remote push servers are involved.

Third-Party Services

The app contains no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13 and collects no user data.
